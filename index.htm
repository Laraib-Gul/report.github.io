1.  Abstract

 

Due to covid-19 people were unable to plan their tours. People were confined to their houses as many travel agencies were closed. The issues people faced in the last 2 years, made their minds frozen. Now, People don't want to waste more time in selecting the best travel medium or deciding the popular areas to visit that match their interests. If they want to go on tour then they have to manage things physically which requires massive effort. These things are quite irritating to be handled at the user's end. Therefore we are creating such a website where the users can manage all these tasks online by leaving the planning of their journey on the intelligent system. Our system will be extremely helpful in reducing the tour planning burden of people. The online website will help users to save time and physical effort. It is user-friendly, user-dependent, and self-explanatory. Tourado'', an online platform, is designed especially for foreigners who are present in Pakistan. It also helps literate people to plan their tours online who live in rural areas and want to visit Pakistan but they have come to the city for the booking as there is no efficient and satisfactory agency. In Tourado, users will be able to see popular Pakistani areas along with famous restaurants, hotels, the best traveling mediums & nearby hospitals. Users will be able to plan their tour according to their budget. Packages may vary in prices. After booking a trip, Tourado will calculate total expenses regarding the tour and display it to users. This system is providing us an academic benefit as it is a requirement of our degree and we have to clear it out.

2.  Background and Justification

 

We explored six websites Tripco [1], Tripadvisor [2], Ctrip.com [3], Cheapflights [4], Pakistan Tour & Travels [5], and Tripit [6]. In Tripco, the users were able to see the schedule from departure to return, reserve seats, see the total expense of the tour, search the available tour plan, customize their trip, and see their booked tours on their dashboard. In Tripadvisor, the users were able to sign up/log in, save ideas of places they want to visit later, find nearby hotels, view Google maps, see the recommendation of restaurants, see Tripadvisor forums, choose traveling medium, and give feedback. In Ctrip.com, users were able to sign up/login to avail the facilities and also save up to 50%, choose language and currency type, book hotels, reserve seats, do service chat, earn the extra rewards by downloading the app, get recommendations or search manually for places, choose membership tiers to avail special offers and give feedback. In Cheapflights, users were able to change language and currency type, book flights, book hotels, book flights plus hotels together in one package, and also find out the best rental car prices. In Pakistan Tour & Travels, users were offered the best tour packages, car rental options, special packages for foreigners, also they could see blogs, and customize their plans. In Tripit, users could sign up and also download the app, book the tours. Tripit also offered a Pro version for the users, consisting of some extra charges. We found the following limitations in these websites:

No nearby hospitals and doctors were recommended in case of emergency, these websites did not recommend essentials which the user should carry with himself, users were not able to view the deals without signing up in Cheapflights, users were unpleasant from picking their desired tour plans in Pakistan Tour & Travels as it had too much text and they had to scroll a lot to find the exact options, no use of graphics, if users edit the dates of the tour using Tripit then the website should show the updated dates but Tripit took users back to the beginning of website which was quite annoying for users to scroll down and find updated plan, could not delete duplicate entry within the trip only way was to delete entire plan and start over, no confirmation of seat booking, even if users only wanted to check list of places for road trips users were forced to input lots of details and in-depth info, could not see anything without signing up, too many verifications were required to access the website, no suggestions of plans for the users, therefore, they had to customize everything themselves.

3.  Project Methodology

 

Users can sign up, in order to avail the facilities provided by Tourado. Users can fill out a questionnaire according to their interest. Best tour packages will be shown to the users selected according to the options they have selected in the questionnaire. An intelligent algorithm will suggest the necessary clothes the users should carry according to the weather of the selected areas. Users can reserve rooms in hotels. They can select a travel medium according to their interest. They can book a car and jeep according to their budget and family members. They can also travel by train or by air. Users can search tourist spots manually, see the exact location through Google maps and customize their trip. Users can see  nearby hospitals. These hospitals will be located in the city where users will be currently present during their travel. Users can chat with the system and ask questions in case of any confusion. Users can make payments through Jazz Cash Account, Easypaisa Account, Bank Account or Paypal Account. Users can see the reviews of other folks about those areas that users will visit. They can give their feedback.

Model

 

The model that we adopted in our project is the Waterfall model [4]. The Waterfall Model was the first Process Model to be introduced. It is also referred to as a linear-sequential life cycle model. It is very simple to understand and use. In "The Waterfall" approach, the whole process of software development is divided into separate phases. Each phase must be completed before the next phase can begin and there is no overlapping in the phases. In this model, typically, the outcome of one phase acts as the input for the next phase sequentially.

10.     Non-functional Requirements

Performance Requirements

The Tourado website should be able to respond to the queries submitted by the customer without much delay. When a client looks for a visiting area, the website should not take much time to return the results... Considering that the website is of moderate size, it should be able to display 10 results at a time on each page, when the client searches up for a specific information. In case of excess traffic, the user should also be able to log in to the system using high speed internet. Most of the requests sent to the Tourado website should be answered in less than 5 seconds.

Safety Requirements

The database may get crashed or damaged due to some viruses or operating system requirements. Therefore, it is necessary to have backup for your data. The UPS/Inverter facility should be there in case of power failure. If a large portion of the database is corrupted due to a serious problem, such as a disk failure, the recovery method will restore the previous copy of the database that was backed up to storage and recreate a more current state. By reapplying committed transaction operations  from the saved log, up to the point of failure.

 

Security Requirements

It must be ensured that access will be provided to the authorized persons through user ID and password. Network security will be provided by the use of firewalls. Checks can be performed at regular intervals to ensure data integrity. All internal data should not reveal the identity of the operator. The CBT (Computer Based Training) file should be restricted to those who either personally request the training program or those who are intended to use it for the project exercise. Websites should not reveal any publicly identifiable information of the user.

User Documentation

We will deliver a user manual and video tutorial along with the project. The user manual will have all the description of how to use Tourado website, list of facilities provided by the Tourado website, description of how users can avail these facilities, list of operations, and description of how users can perform these operations. The online video tutorial will be provided to understand the working environment of the Tourado website so that users can easily understand the working of the Tourado website.

13.    Class Diagram

 

Figure 6: Class Diagram

 

Description:

User: This class includes the following attributes that will be stored by the user in the database: Users ID(Primary Key) of int type, Users Name of string type, Users Email of string type, Users Password of string type, Users Phone Number of int type, Users CNIC of int type and Users Address of string type. The function AddUserDetails() is used to add the details of the user in the database, ModifyUserDetails() is used to modify user's details in the database, DeleteUserDetails() is used to delete the user's details in the database.

Sign Up: This class includes the following attributes that will be stored by the user in the database: Users Name of string type, Users Email of string type, Users Password and Retype Password of string type, Users Phone Number of int type, Users current-location of string type, Users Address of string type. The function SignUp() is used to sign up the user and AddSignUpDetails() is used to add the sign-up details of the user in the database.

Log In: This class includes the following attributes that will be given by the user and match with the sign up data which is stored in the database: Users Email of string type and Users Password of int type. The function LogIn() is used to Login the user and VerifyUser() is used to verify entered details of the user.

Area: This class includes the following attributes that are stored in the database: Area ID (Primary Key) of int type, Area Name of string type and Province Name of string type. This class also has some secondary keys like User ID of int type and City ID of int type. The function DisplayAreas() is used to display popular areas of a City and StoreSelectedAreas() is used to store the users selected areas in the database.

14.    Sequence Diagram

Figure 7: Sequence Diagram

 

Description:

The first thing which appears on screen is the Homepage when a user clicks on the website Tourado. So when a user clicks on the Tourado Website, a request to access Home Page will be generated and after proceeding with the user's request the Home Page will be visible to the user. The user can view the website without signing up but to plan a tour users must have to sign up. When a user clicks on Plan Tour, a request to plan a tour will be generated and after proceeding with the user's request, a Sign Up form will be visible to the user. If the user is new then he must enter his details in Sign Up form and if the user is old then he must enter Login Details. If the user is an old one then he will request access to the Log In Page, after proceeding with the user's request a LogIn Page will appear to the user. After Login to the website Tourado, the user can plan his tour through exploring packages, filling questionnaires and getting recommendations from the System and the user can also customize his tour.

If the user selects the Packages Button: If the user clicks on Packages then a request to view packages will generate and after processing this request, multiple Packages will appear on screen. The user will be able to select the package according to his desire. After selecting the desired package, the user will select the medium through which he wants to travel. In order to view traveling mediums, a request will be generated and after processing the user's request, possible traveling mediums will be visible to the user. At the end the user will request for his billing information, after proceeding this request, billing information will be visible to the user. Then the user will confirm his plan by making payment through Jazz Cash, Easy Paisa or PayPal Account.

If the user selects the Get Recommendations Button: If the user clicks on Get Recommendations then a request to display a Questionnaire will be generated and after processing this request a Questionnaire will appear on screen. After filling the Questionnaire, a request to get recommendations will be generated and recommendations will appear on screen. User can select recommended tour plans according to his desire. Then, to select traveling mediums, a request will be generated and after processing the user's request, possible traveling mediums will be visible to the user. At the end the user will request for his billing information, after processing this request, billing information will be visible to the user. Then the user will confirm his plan by making payment through Jazz Cash, Easy Paisa or PayPal Account.

If the user selects the Customize Trip Button: If the user clicks on Customize Trip then a request to customize a trip will generate and after proceeding with this request all places including restaurants, hotels, popular areas will be visible to the user. After selecting desired places, hotels and restaurants where the user wants to visit, a request for selecting a traveling medium will be generated and after processing the user's request, possible traveling mediums will be visible to the user. At the end the user will request for his billing information, after processing this request, billing information will be visible to the user. Then the user will confirm his plan by making payment through Jazz Cash, Easy Paisa or PayPal Account.

If a user is facing some issue while operating the website Tourado or he has some confusion regarding something then he can request for live chat with the administrator. After processing the user's request, a platform for Live Chat will appear on screen.

If a user needs to contact the website Tourado through Facebook, Gmail, Instagram, Twitter and Phone Number. The user will request access to contact details and after processing the user's request, all details will be provided to the user.

If user is curious to know about Tourado then he may click on About Us. A request to access about will be generated and Tourado information will be displayed to user.

If a user wants to read others reviews regarding different places then he may click on Travel Forum. When a user clicks on Travel Forum, a request to access forums will be generated and the platform will be visible to the user.

Class of our Software Project: Organic

Stages of COCOMO:

  1. Basic:

Basic COCOMO Equations

●        Effort Applied (E) = a(KLoC)b

where E is the effort applied in person months, KLoC is the estimated number of thousands of delivered lines of code for the project.

●        Development Time (D) = c(Effort Applied)d

The Development time D calculation uses E.

●        People Required (P) = E/D

And also the most effective number of person P calculation uses E and D.
